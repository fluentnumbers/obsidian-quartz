# [[PKM]]
## Note
- A form of Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) based on the idea that frequent publishing of *atomic* notes helps in organizing thoughts. Although. also a form of blogging it does not evolve into a set of chronological and *complete* posts. It is more akin to a personal [[wikipedia]] as in “*writing the content you wish you could have found before*”.
- It is personal, imperfect, evolving, diverse in content, not temporarily ordered or restricted.
